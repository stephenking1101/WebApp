Task: Collect Detailed Business Requirements

Purpose

Based on the agreed scope and business requirements, define the range of requirements needed for the delivery of a high quality solution. These may include:

  • Functional requirements
  • Non-functional requirements
  • Customer Experience requirements
  • Service & Data requirements
  • Identify Operational Risks
  • Group Guidance Considerations – Group Manual System, FIM etc

Procedure

  1. Review Business Requirements
  2. Collect the detailed business requirements from the Business Representative using techniques such as Brainstroming, facilitated workshops etc.
  3. Evaluate and proritise requirements
  4. Review and validate the requirements with key stakeholders

While evaluating the requirements, refer to Considerations for assessing impact to identify any requirements which could impact (positively and negatively) the sustainability goals.

Relationships

Outputs
  • Business Requirements
