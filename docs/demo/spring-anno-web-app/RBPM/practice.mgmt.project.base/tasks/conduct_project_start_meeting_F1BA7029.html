Task: Conduct Project Start Briefing

Purpose

It is vital that Project Manager fully understand the objective and goals of the project and the decisions taken during Project Initiation.
  1. Schedule a briefing for the project, including all key stakeholders, such as the Sponsor, Business Representative, all assigned Staff and any Subject Experts who may be required.
  2. In advance of the meeting, distribute copies of the Investment Summary (Draft) to all attendees, allowing sufficient time for a thorough review of the document.
  3. At the kick-off meeting, use the format most appropriate to the project. This may be:
    • Presentation by the Project Manager/Business Representative
    • Formal walk-through of the Investment Summary document
    • Informal question and answer session
  4. Ensure that any clarifications or decisions are properly documented. It is conceivable that discussions at the meeting could lead to modification of the requirements. Any such modifications must be incorporated in the Investment Summary document and approved by the Project Sponsor and key stakeholders identified.
