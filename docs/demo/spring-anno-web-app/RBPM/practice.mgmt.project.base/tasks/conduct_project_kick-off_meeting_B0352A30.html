Task: Conduct Project Kick-off Meeting

Purpose

It is vital that key project team members fully understand the objective and goals of the project and their roles and responsibilities within the project.
  1. Schedule a kick-off meeting for the project, including all core project team members, such as the Sponsor, Business Representative, all assigned Staff and any Subject Experts who may be required.
  2. In advance of the meeting, distribute copies of the Investment Summary to all project team members, allowing sufficient time for a thorough review of the document.
  3. At the kick-off meeting, use the format most appropriate to the project. This may be:
    • Presentation by the Project Manager/User Representative
    • Formal walk-through of the Investment Summary document
    • Informal question and answer session  

Key Considerations

Ensure that any clarifications or decisions are properly documented. It is conceivable that discussions at the meeting could lead to modification of the requirements.
