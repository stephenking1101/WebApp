Task: Assess Stakeholder Satisfaction

Purpose

The stakeholder satisfaction survey measurement process explores how well expectations met and drives continuous improvement on the engagement. Distribute stakeholder satisfaction surveys to customers, clients, end users, employees, and third parties such as suppliers, partners, and vendors.
The satisfaction survey focuses on people who interact with the projects on a regular basis. Establish a list of key stakeholders to include in the survey. Send a survey to a cross-representative sub-set of the list each quarter. Outline coverage targets for each survey including expected response rates. For example, 100% of employees are surveyed and the expected response rate is 50%.
