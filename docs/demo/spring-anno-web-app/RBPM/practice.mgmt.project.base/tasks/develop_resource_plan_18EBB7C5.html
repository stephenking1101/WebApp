Task: Develop Resource Plan

Purpose

To determine the resources required to complete the project work and how and where to secure the resources.

Procedure

  1. Define the roles and skill sets required to complete the project work
  2. Determine the timetable for the required staff and check availability
  3. Secure the resources
  4. Identify any training or coaching requirements

Key Considerations

  • As the project schedule is developed, adjustments may be required to the resource plan.
  • The understanding of the skills needed to complete the project is essential to proper resource selection. When introducing new technology, it is often a good idea to employ temporary (contract) staff with skills in the new technology to provide knowledge transfer for the rest of the team. If this is the reason for using external resources, it is essential to ensure the knowledge transfer is actually completed during the contract period.
  • The most effective teams are those with a mix of skills that align to the skills demanded within the project. It is therefore good practice to select individuals that have skills that are complementary to your own and those of existing team members. In other words, select people who can do the things you and the rest of the team are not good at.
