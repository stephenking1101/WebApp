Task: Refine / Validate Business (High Level) Requirements

Purpose

Following the discussions on scope of the project, the business may chose to further refine / validate the project scope, in which case the Business (High Level) Requirements will need to be revised.

Procedure

  1. Review Project Scope
  2. Collect the requirements from the Business Representative and other important stakeholders using techniques such as Brainstroming, facilitated workshops etc.
  3. Evaluate and proritise requirements
  4. Identify the requirements which are in scope and out of scope for the project
  5. Review and validate the requirements with key stakeholders

While evaluating the requirements, refer to Considerations for assessing impact to identify any requirements which could impact (positively and negatively) the sustainability goals.
