Task: Verify Project Deliverables

Purpose

Ensure that all deliverables, appropriate to the project technical domain, deliver all the functionality defined in the Business Requirements and achieve the level of quality defined in the project Quality plan

Approach

Review and Approve Build Deliverables consists of two stages,

  • Build Deliverable Review is conducted by a team of project stakeholders with the requisite technical skills in the technical domain to validate that the deliverables are of sufficient quality and that they deliver the functionality to the customer as defined in the Business Requirements. It may be accomplished on all deliverables at once, on individual deliverables as they are completed or in phases or groups of deliverables as appropriate to the project plan.
  • Build Deliverables Approval is a final review of all approved Build Deliverables that are to be implemented in the current project phase. It is accomplished in a meeting chaired by the Project Manager and attended by key stakeholders representing customers, design team(s), build team(s) and implementation/installation team(s). Other stakeholder groups (e.g., HOST Security, Compliance, Legal, Regulatory agencies, etc.) may participate based on their understanding of the project deliverables and how they are to be used within the customer organization or by customers individually.
