Milestone: Execution Tollgate

Check if the project is on the right track to deliver the agreed benefits / goals.

Make the decision on whether to reject the project, loop to redo elements of execution (agreed at the meeting), or proceed to next stage

Implementation Exit
  • Implementation is complete to all in scope business areas and countries.
  • Implementation is complete to all in scope sites and teams within these business areas and countries.
  • There are no active implementation issues.
Solution
  • The in scope business requirements and solution have been implemented in full.
  • There are no defects with the solution or there is a fix release being executed and to complete before full embedding starts.
Change
  • Feedback from implementation has been acted on.  Any new impacts or changes have been identified and being managed.
  • Audiences have good awareness/understanding and have accepted the change.  Where necessary they are showing ownership and commitment to the change.
  • There is confidence to move to embedding change.  Audiences are ready and willing to adopt and use the change solution. 
  • If applicable, the change network is still in place and actively engaged in managing the change.
  • If applicable, training is being delivered to audiences, including the delivery of non-training learning interventions, such as coaching or support from super users.
  • If applicable, organisation alignment and operations alignment changes have been completed (e.g. process changes, job description changes etc.).
  • Plans for increasing adoption and usage are in place.
  • Plans for benefits realisation are in place and actions are being put in place.
  • Tracking and reporting of the change and benefits is active. The project is on track against benefits targets for the current project milestones. Areas of variance are being acted on.
  • All stakeholders (including sponsor) are still in place and actively engaged.
Plan, Budget, Risks and Issues
  • The project is on track against plan. Any variance has been managed.
  • The project is on track against budget. Any variance has been managed.
  • Project risks and issues have been actively managed.
  • Document evidence that the checklists have been applied. Completion of the checklists may be audited.
Transition and Closure
  • There is funding available for the Change, Transition & Closure phase.
  • There is a clear plan for the Change, Transition & Closure phase. Responsibilities and milestone dates have been communicated.
  • Resource requirements for Change, Transition & Closure have been raised and all resources/teams are being mobilised. Execution teams have been demobilised where appropriate.
  • There is adequate sponsor and business stakeholder involvement for the Change, Transition & Closure phase. Business responsibilities for embedding are clear.
  • Handover activities to start to move the change towards full business ownership have been identified.
  • Project closure criteria (agreed during Definition) have been re-communicated and readiness activities towards these outcomes is underway.
  • All project documents are updated and signed off as required.
