Milestone: Closure Tollgate

Check if the project is on the right track to deliver the agreed benefits / goals

Make the decision on whether loop to redo elements of closure (agreed at the meeting).

Transition
  • There has been full knowledge transfer from the project team to the business.
  • All documents have been transferred to the business.
  • All sponsor and stakeholder sign-offs for project deliverables have been stored.
  • The business executive owners have been formally passed responsibility for the change.
  • Business teams are fully operational and responsibilities clear.
  • All processes are being operated in business as usual model.
  • All systems have helpdesk and application maintenance support.
  • All people processes have been transitioned to HR.  All training has been repackaged and embedded into induction and for business as usual use.
Closure Activity
  • All project resources have rolled off.
  • Any underspent budget has been reallocated/saved.
  • Learning for future change projects has been documented.
  • Knowledge assets have been harvested and turned into reusable assets where appropriate.
  • A Post Implementation Review has taken place.
  • A short project 'success story' has been written up and communicated within the Transformation team and other delivery partners.
  • Prior to setting the project as closed in Clarity and archiving the repository, perform a review of all documents stored in the repository to ensure all project management documents are uploaded, completed and signed off.
  • Review the repository to ensure that any highly restricted documents have been correctly classified and stored appropriately.
Readiness for Benefits Realisation Phase
  • Ongoing benefits realisation activities have been planned following the project.
  • Benefits targets, milestones and ownership have been communicated.
  • Change and benefits tracking have been transferred to Finance and the business.
  • For larger projects, an initial benefits realisation review has been scheduled for 6-12 months time.
